Key Meeting Types

Full Council

All 39 councillors meet to make key decisions including setting the budget and agreeing major policies.

Frequency: Usually monthly

Time: Typically 6:00pm

Cabinet

The Cabinet makes most of the council's major decisions on services and policy.

Frequency: Usually monthly

Time: Typically 6:00pm

Planning Committee

Decides on planning applications that can't be decided by officers.

Frequency: Usually fortnightly

Time: Typically 6:00pm

Overview and Scrutiny Committee

Holds the Cabinet to account and reviews council policies and decisions.

Frequency: Usually monthly

Time: Typically 6:00pm
